Plantin Moretuslei

This quiet, historic square is located in the heart of Antwerp, near the iconic Plantin-Moretus Museum. Once the centre of the global printing press industry, it still exudes an atmosphere of craftsmanship and creativity. The square is a place where time seems to stand still and history is palpable.

Scent Diffuser: The distinctive scent of ink and aged paper fills the air, evoking the rich history of the printing press.

Sensory Item: Feel the weight of the metal printing press letters in your hands, experiencing the craftsmanship behind each character.

Sound: The rhythmic clatter of a working printing press echoes around you, transporting you to the heart of an old print shop.
