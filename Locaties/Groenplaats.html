Groenplaats

Located in the historic city centre near the Cathedral of Our Lady, Groenplaats is a lively square filled with history. Originally a cemetery, it was transformed into a public square in the late 18th century. Today, it is surrounded by cafés and restaurants, making it a popular spot for locals and visitors alike.

Scent Diffuser: The aroma of fresh coffee from nearby cafés.

Sensory Item: Feel the cool touch of a historic stone bench.

Sound: The chatter of people and distant cathedral bells.
