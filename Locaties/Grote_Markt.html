Grote Markt

This central square is surrounded by beautiful 16th and 17th century guild houses and the imposing town hall. At its heart is the Brabofontein, which depicts the legend of Silvius Brabo. The Grote Markt is a popular place for tourists and locals alike because of its historic architecture and lively atmosphere.

Scent Diffuser: The sweet aroma of freshly baked Antwerpse Handjes cookies, with hints of butter and almonds, fills the air.

Sensory Item: Hold and feel the delicate texture of a chocolate or biscuit Antwerpse Handje, crafted in the shape of a hand.

Sound: The soft crunch of a biscuit as you take a bite, paired with the lively chatter of a bustling Antwerp bakery.
