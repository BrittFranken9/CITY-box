Welcome to City Box

Welcome to our interactive journey through Antwerp! This app is designed to help you explore the city in a unique way, by engaging your senses through special "boxes" placed at iconic locations. Each box offers a multi-sensory experience, making you connect with the city in a deeper way.

Scent Diffuser – A typical scent of the place, like coffee, chocolate, or the fresh aroma of a garden.

Sensory Item – Something you can touch, look at, or taste that represents the location.

Sound – A sound related to the environment, such as city buzz or nature sounds.

Elevated Experience – A box that adds height, enhancing your interaction with the experience.

The app guides you to different locations, such as the Grote Markt, Groenplaats, and the Botanical Garden, where you can discover these boxes. You can also track your progress and see if a box is still in working condition.

As we expand, more locations will be added, allowing you to continue your exploration of Antwerp.

Discover, experience, and explore Antwerp like never before!
